Reference piano samples
=======================

The note recordings in this folder (C3–B3, one per chromatic note) are from the
tonejs-instruments sample pack:

    https://github.com/nbrosowsky/tonejs-instruments  (piano)

License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC-BY 3.0).
Per that pack's notes, the underlying recordings come from a variety of
public-domain sources.

File names use the app's own scheme (e.g. Db.mp3 = C#3, Gb.mp3 = F#3) so the
front-end <audio id="..."> mapping is unchanged.
