Fretboard instrument samples
=============================

The guitar (`guitar/`) and bass (`bass/`) note recordings used by the Fretboard
panel are a curated subset of the tonejs-instruments sample pack:

    https://github.com/nbrosowsky/tonejs-instruments
    (guitar-acoustic and bass-electric)

License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC-BY 3.0).
Per that pack's notes, the underlying recordings come from a variety of
public-domain sources.

In Loopretto these are pitch-shifted (via Web Audio playbackRate) to cover every
fret, so only a handful of anchor notes per instrument are bundled.
