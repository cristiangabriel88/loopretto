How to Use Loopretto

  • 🎧 Paste a YouTube URL in the input field and click Load Audio.
  • 🔊 Click anywhere on the waveform to set a start point. Then press the Spacebar to play. Press the Spacebar again to pause and come back to the place you clicked at initially.
  • 🔁 Click anywhere on the waveform to set a start point. Then click Loop section to loop a segment and use the mouse to drag the ends of the segment.
  • ⏩ Use the speed controls to slow down or speed up playback.
  • 🎹 Toggle the Piano view to find notes by ear. You can play it with your keyboard: Z to M for white keys, and S, D, G, H, J for black keys.
  • 📥 You can download the audio using the download button.
  • 🧠 Activate Focus Mode for a fullscreen transcription session.

Loopretto runs right in your browser — no installations, no ads, no clutter. Just music and tools that help you learn by ear — better and faster.
