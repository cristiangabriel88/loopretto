How to use

Loop it. Slow it. Nail it.

Six moves — that's the whole app.

01

Drop a link

Paste a YouTube, SoundCloud or Bandcamp link — or drag in a file.

02

Mark the spot

Click the waveform where you want to start.

03

Loop a phrase

Hit Loop, then drag the edges to fit.

04

Slow it down

Tap Speed −. The pitch stays put.

05

Find the notes

Open the Piano, or Notes to read the root.

06

Drill it

Metronome, drone, reps & memorize live in Practice.
