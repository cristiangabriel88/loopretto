About Loopretto

Loopretto is a sleek and simple web app designed for musicians and curious ears. Just drop in a YouTube link, and Loopretto extracts the audio, giving you an intuitive, distraction-free space to zoom in, loop sections, slow down playback, download the song and even jam along using the built-in piano.

Whether you're transcribing a jazz solo, studying tricky licks, or just looping a beat for fun — Loopretto helps you hear more and guess less.

Key Features

  • 🎧 Extracts high-quality audio from any YouTube video
  • 🔁 Set loop regions with ease
  • 🐢 Slow down or speed up playback without losing pitch
  • 🎹 Virtual piano for pitch matching and reference
  • 🌙 Zen mode for fullscreen, focused listening
  • 🎨 Custom themes to match your vibe

Loopretto runs right in your browser — no installations, no ads, no clutter. Just music and tools that help you learn by ear — better and faster.
