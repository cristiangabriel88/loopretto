About

Hear more.
Guess less.

A clean, local workspace for learning music by ear.

Drop in a link and Loopretto pulls the audio into a distraction-free space — loop any phrase, slow it down without changing pitch, transpose the key, find the root, and jam along on the built-in piano. Whether you're transcribing a solo or just chasing a lick, it helps you learn by feel.

Loop any section
Slow down, same pitch
Transpose the key
Root + reference piano
Metronome & drone
100% local & private

Built by Cristian Gabriel — a musician & developer. It runs entirely on your machine: no accounts, no uploads, nothing leaves your computer.
